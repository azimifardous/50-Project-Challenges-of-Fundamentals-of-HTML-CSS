How to play? ðŸ˜‰

  • By pressing the play button in the home page, you're going to be referred to another page, where you have to select one of the categories that are listed there. Which are Movie related quizzes, Technology related quizzes, and Anime related quizzes.
  • By selecting any one of the categories out there, you are going to start answering each category's questions.
  • Each category has 10 questions, the hardness and easiness of the questions are not based on their respective numbers.
  • You have to select one and only single answer for each question.
  • You cannot go to the next question unless you submit and check your answer.
  • After you click on the check button, the result is going to be shown, the correct answer is going to get a green background and the rest that are incorrect are going to have a red background.
  • It doesn't matter which answer is selected, whether it would be correct or incorrect, it has a darker text color, which you can differentiate from the rest.
  • After you are done with answering every question out there, your result and score will be shown there. However, if you don't answer anything correctly, you won't have any score at all. But you can try again.
  • Note: After finishing and playing again your recent data and result won't save. It's going to start over again.
